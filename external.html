Culture and Traditions of Almaty

Almaty is not only the economic and educational center of Kazakhstan but also a city with rich culture and traditions. Here, ancient Kazakh customs intertwine with modern lifestyles.

The city is famous for its museums, theaters, and art galleries. One of the key cultural landmarks is the Abay State Opera and Ballet Theater.

Almaty is also known for its unique cuisine, where you can try traditional Kazakh dishes such as beshbarmak, kazy, and baursaks.
